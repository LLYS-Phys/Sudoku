Gameplay:

  • Click New Game to select a difficulty level and whether you would like to be notified for any mistakes
  • Click Reset Board to restart your current board
  • Click on any free cell to enter the number you think should be there
  • Click on any cell that you have already filled to edit the number in it (if you click away, your previously entered number would remain there)
  • Double click on any cell that you have filled with a number to delete that number
